# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone contributing to or using MicroEngine, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Maintain professional conduct in all project interactions

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

- Use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Sustained disruption of discussions or collaboration

## Technical Standards

In addition to behavioral standards, all contributions must adhere to:

- The technical guidelines in `CONTRIBUTING.md`
- The architectural principles in `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md`
- The coding standards defined in `.github/copilot-instructions.md`

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and documentation
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Project communication channels (discussions, forums, chat)
- Official project events and meetings
- Any public space where an individual represents the project

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at:

- **Email:** [project contact email]
- **GitHub:** Open a confidential issue or contact maintainers directly

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All project maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

### Consequences

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject:

- Comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct
- Contributors who display inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful behavior

Consequences for violations may include:

1. **Warning:** A private or public warning about the behavior
2. **Temporary Ban:** Temporary restriction from project interaction
3. **Permanent Ban:** Permanent removal from the project community

The severity of consequences depends on the nature and frequency of the violation.

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct, please open an issue or contact the project maintainers.
